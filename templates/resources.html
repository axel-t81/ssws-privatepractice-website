Here you'll find helpful phone numbers and links to support your journey.

Important Phone Numbers

  • Mental Health Triage Line: 1800 629 354
    Free call for mental health support
  • Domestic Violence Crisis Service (DVCS): (02) 6280 0900
    24/7 support for domestic violence
  • Griefline: 1300 845 745
    Support for grief and loss
  • 13YARN: 13 92 76
    Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander culturally appropriate support
  • Lifeline: 13 11 14
    24/7 crisis support and suicide prevention services
  • ACT Health Intake: 02 5124 9977
    Mental health support services in ACT
  • ACT Policing: 131 444
    Non-emergency police assistance
